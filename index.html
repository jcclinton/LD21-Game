You are the legendary hero Spartacus and your soldiers have been captured by the evil romans!

Jail
Freedom

But the Roman Guards are going to try stop you. They will kill your soldiers if you don't get them first!

Guard

The goal of the game is to move as many soldiers to the exit before time runs out.


Instructions:

Drag Spartacus with the mouse to move him. You can also use the arrow keys to move him (but you must click him first).

To help your soldiers, you must set waypoints from the jail. To set a waypoint, drag the jail to where you want your soldiers to go. You may place 3 waypoints. Once you add your first waypoint, drag the green box to add another.

  • If Spartacus comes near a Roman guard, he will kill him
  • If a Roman guard comes near one of your soldiers, he will kill the soldier
  • If a soldier reaches the exit, he escapes and you get a point
Waypoint

Tips:

  • Spartacus can't move as fast as the guards, use your waypoints to get around them when Spartacus is not around!
  • Spartacus has a bigger kill range than the guards, use this to block guards from your escaping inmates.

Notes:

  • Helping soldiers escape will infuriate the guards! The more that have escaped, the more guards will be sent.
  • On the other hand, helping soldiers escape will give your soldiers a morale boost and will extend your time.
  • The outline of the game is a brown line, and it also bisects the gameboard in two places.
  • Units cannot move outside the brown outline, that is out-of-bounds.
  • guards will never spawn to the left of the first safety zone (ie the left brown line)
  • there can only be so many guards that are active at once, once their ranks are full, they stop spawning
  • guards are inherently lazy, and they will lose interest after a while. Once this happens they will leave and new guards will come to take their place.
